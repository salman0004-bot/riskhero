Frequently asked questions

How does your library of assets help my risk management program?

Imagine having access to all commonly used and industry-recognized control frameworks, risk catalogs, and risk assessments at your fingertips. No more wasted cycles searching for risk management content — leave the heavy lifting to us. Harness the power of the RiskHero library to save time and work faster.

We have our own risk ratings and definitions — can RiskHero use our custom definitions?

Users can change risk ratings and definitions by category within the platform. This allows you to tailor RiskHero to your company's internal framework.

Can I bring existing historical assessments, control frameworks, and risk registers into RiskHero?

RiskHero professional services can help bring all of this information into the platform for your company's account. Reach out to our support team to begin the process.

How many people can respond to an assessment at once?

Assessments can be routed to multiple people at once — inside or outside your organization. There is no limit to how many people can respond or how many times you ask them to respond. The assessment owner sees all responses come in real time through one seamless platform experience.

Can I change my plan after signing up?

We allow users to upgrade and cancel at any time. No questions asked.

Do you offer integration with other software solutions?

RiskHero is working with key partners to offer API integrations with commonly used SaaS solutions to complement your Risk Management program. Join our mailing list below for updates.
