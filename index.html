Frequently asked questions

How does your library of assets help my risk management program?

Imagine having access to all commonly used and industry-recognized control frameworks, risk catalogs, and risk assessments at your fingertips. No more wasted cycles searching for risk management content — leave the heavy lifting to us.

We have our own risk ratings and definitions — can RiskHero use our custom definitions?

Users can change risk ratings and definitions by category within the platform. This allows you to tailor RiskHero to your company's internal framework.

Can I bring existing historical assessments, control frameworks, and risk registers into RiskHero?

RiskHero professional services can help bring all of this information into the platform. Reach out to our support team to begin the process.

How many people can respond to an assessment at once?

Assessments can be routed to multiple people at once — inside or outside your organization. There is no limit to how many people can respond. The assessment owner sees all responses in real time through one seamless experience.

Can I change my plan after signing up?

We allow users to upgrade and cancel at any time. No questions asked.

Do you offer integration with other software solutions?

RiskHero is working with key partners to offer API integrations with commonly used SaaS solutions. Join our mailing list below for updates.

About RiskHero

Built by risk professionals, for the AI era

RiskHero was founded on the belief that taking measured risks separates greatness from mediocrity. With the right governance and focus, organizations can maximize their potential — especially as AI transforms how work gets done.

Today, RiskHero is the AI Governance & Assurance Platform that helps organizations assess whether their AI systems are operating as designed and meeting regulatory requirements. We sit at the intersection of enterprise risk management and the emerging AI compliance landscape — complementing technical trust infrastructure providers like Mnemom to give organizations a complete picture of their AI risk posture.

With over 40 years of combined ERM expertise behind the platform, RiskHero empowers companies of every size to manage risk intentionally — and prove it to the people who matter most.
